About

Hello! I am Ahmad Mahmod. PhD Student at Networks Team, ICube Laboratory, Strasbourg University.

My main research interest is programmable networks using Software Defined Network (SDN) specialized in IoT networks.

I have an experience with Embedded Systems and Machine Learning especially Federated Learning.
